About
reap medical

Reap Medical, established in 2019, is a thriving player in Egypt's medical aesthetics market. Our founders envisioned a transformative approach to the aesthetic industry. In 2022, we split into two entities: Reap Medical Consumables and Reap Medical Devices. The former specializes in aesthetic and cosmetic consumables, while the latter focuses on medical devices for dermatology, gynecology, and whole-body aesthetic treatments.

we are on a mission to be the market leaders in the aesthetics market.
